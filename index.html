11 · Why now

Why this matters in 2026.

Buyers are no longer moving linearly from awareness to sales call. They are checking Google, asking AI tools, reading category pages, comparing vendors, scanning LinkedIn, validating proof, and only then deciding who is worth a conversation. That creates a risk and an opportunity.

The risk

Competitors with weaker proof shape the shortlist first.

Zappi, Suzy, Kantar, Qualtrics, and Attest are publishing volume across search, AI answers, review sites, and comparison pages. Their content is generic but it is everywhere. If Swayable does not enter those surfaces deliberately, AI systems and buyers will keep assembling shortlists that do not include Swayable - even when Swayable is the better fit.

The opportunity

Swayable has what competitors cannot manufacture.

Proprietary RCT data, a credible methodology, category-relevant executive voices, and proof that maps directly to the questions modern marketers are asking. These are exactly the inputs AI systems prioritize and buyers trust. The window to translate those assets into compounding visibility is open right now.

!
AI visibility compounds early. Vendors that become part of training data, indexed sources, and frequently cited pages gain disproportionate share of future demand. Delaying this means competitors define the category in AI systems first.
The decision

Swayable already has the proof. The question is whether those assets are consistently turning into pipeline.

Right now, too many buyer-research moments are likely happening without Swayable owning the conversation: competitor searches, category searches, AI answers, review-site checks, comparison pages, and high-intent paid queries. We build the system that closes that gap.

The first 90 days are designed to prove the model: ship the highest-intent pages, validate demand with paid search, connect reporting to demo activity, and turn existing proof into assets that sales and marketing can reuse. If the system shows qualified demand, we scale it. If something does not convert, we know quickly and redirect budget. That is the operating model: test, prove, scale. The same principle Swayable gives its customers.
