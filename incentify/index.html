03 · The Two Sides

Strong foundation. Weak execution. Fixable gap.

Incentify's credibility layer is genuinely well-built. The brand has real enterprise proof, clear category positioning, and educational content depth - assets that many competitors lack. The execution gaps are structural, formatting, and measurement problems. Fully fixable with focused effort.

What is strong

Credibility layer is genuinely well-built.

These strengths are the foundation everything else should be built on. We do not need to recreate them.

  • Clear category positioningIncentify clearly presents itself as a tax credit and incentive platform on both the homepage and About page. AI systems can cleanly identify the brand's category - a foundational requirement for consistent mention in relevant queries.
  • Strong educational supportThe Resources page explains what Credits & Incentives is and what Incentify specifically does. AI systems get rich, well-structured background content for informational queries.
  • Enterprise proof layerCase studies name 3M, Cargill, Kroger, Tyson, Paramount, and Wyndham. A powerful authority signal AI systems recognize as evidence of real-world credibility and scale.
  • Meaningful product depthExplore, Resources, Case Studies, Blog, News, and Developer / API sections give AI systems multiple entry points to understand the full scope of the platform.
What is weak

Citation depth, formatting, and measurement.

These are not brand problems. They are structural, formatting, and measurement problems that this engagement is built to close.

  • Low AI share-of-voice17 / 100 means Incentify is largely absent from the AI answers its target buyers are receiving.
  • Narrow citation footprintOnly 7 pages are cited by AI systems. A healthy AI-visible site has 20 to 40+ pages contributing. The current concentration is a single point of fragility.
  • Homepage is not answer-firstThe most-visited page contributes less AI value than it should. AI systems struggle to extract clean, direct answers from it.
  • Poor alt-text coverageOnly 19% of media has alt-text. Weakens AI and search engine understanding of visual content.
  • Legacy ERC page still carries visibilityAn outdated signal from a program that has largely sunset. Not ideal for current commercial positioning.
  • Noisy reporting setOff-topic data inflates and distorts opportunity totals. Strategy decisions risk being misaligned with the actual landscape.
